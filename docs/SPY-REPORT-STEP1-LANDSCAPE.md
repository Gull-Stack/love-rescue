- **URL**: https://www.simplepractice.com
- **Pricing**: Starter $49/mo (5 appts), Essential ~$69/mo, Plus ~$99/mo (multi-clinician $74/clinician). Intro offers: $15/mo first year on Starter.

## 2. THERAPIST MARKETPLACE / INSURANCE PLATFORMS

### 2.1 Alma
- **URL**: https://helloalma.com
- **Pricing**: $125/mo or $1,140/yr membership fee. Therapist keeps insurance reimbursement.

- **Therapist Workflow**: Join â†’ get credentialed with insurance panels â†’ receive client referrals from directory â†’ bill through Alma â†’ get paid. Alma handles insurance headaches.
