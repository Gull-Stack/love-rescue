# LoveRescue 16-Week Curriculum

## Philosophy
- **Mirror, not window** — Every module focuses on YOU, not your partner
- **Skills stack progressively** — Each week builds on the last
- **Same truth, multiple angles** — Experts reinforce core principles from different lenses
- **Personalized via assessments** — Your weak areas get extra focus

---

## PHASE 1: FOUNDATION (Weeks 1-4)
*"Know Thyself Before You Can Know Us"*

### Week 1: The Mirror
**Expert:** Course Philosophy + Intro to All Frameworks
**Theme:** You are 100% responsible for your experience
**Skills:**
- Victim vs Creator mindset distinction
- The relationship is a mirror, not a window
- Introduction to the 16-week journey
**Daily Practice:** Catch yourself blaming → reframe to ownership
**Assessment:** Initial relationship satisfaction baseline

### Week 2: Your Attachment Style
**Expert:** Stan Tatkin + Amir Levine (Attached)
**Theme:** Your attachment patterns come from YOUR history
**Skills:**
- Secure, Anxious, Avoidant, Fearful-Avoidant identification
- How YOUR attachment affects YOUR reactions
- Beginning to self-regulate
**Daily Practice:** Notice when you're triggered → identify attachment pattern
**Assessment:** Attachment Style Quiz

### Week 3: Your Love Languages
**Expert:** Gary Chapman
**Theme:** How YOU give and receive love
**Skills:**
- Identify YOUR primary and secondary languages
- Learn to express needs clearly
- Recognize when you're "speaking" wrong language
**Daily Practice:** Give love in your PARTNER'S language, not yours
**Assessment:** Love Languages Quiz

### Week 4: Your Human Needs
**Expert:** Tony Robbins
**Theme:** The 6 needs driving YOUR behavior
**Skills:**
- Certainty, Variety, Significance, Connection, Growth, Contribution
- Which needs dominate YOUR decisions?
- Meeting your own needs (not outsourcing)
**Daily Practice:** Identify which need drives each conflict
**Assessment:** Human Needs Assessment

---

## PHASE 2: COMMUNICATION (Weeks 5-8)
*"How You Say It Matters More Than What You Say"*

### Week 5: The Four Horsemen
**Expert:** John Gottman
**Theme:** YOUR destructive patterns in conflict
**Skills:**
- Criticism, Contempt, Defensiveness, Stonewalling
- Catch YOURSELF deploying horsemen
- The antidotes: Gentle startup, Appreciation, Responsibility, Self-soothing
**Daily Practice:** Log every horseman YOU use → apply antidote
**Assessment:** Four Horsemen Self-Assessment

### Week 6: Tactical Empathy
**Expert:** Chris Voss
**Theme:** Making others feel deeply understood
**Skills:**
- Labeling emotions ("It seems like you're feeling...")
- Mirroring (repeat last 3 words)
- Calibrated questions (How/What, never Why)
**Daily Practice:** Use 3 labels per conversation
**Assessment:** Communication Style Check

### Week 7: Bids for Connection
**Expert:** John Gottman
**Theme:** The small moments that make or break relationships
**Skills:**
- Recognizing bids (often subtle)
- Turning toward vs. turning away vs. turning against
- Making YOUR bids clear and positive
**Daily Practice:** Track bids given/received, turn toward every time
**Assessment:** Bid Response Ratio

### Week 8: Emotional Flooding & Repair
**Expert:** John Gottman + Stan Tatkin
**Theme:** What to do when you're overwhelmed
**Skills:**
- Recognize YOUR flooding signs (heart rate, thoughts racing)
- Self-soothing techniques (pause, breathe, 20-min break)
- Repair attempts (de-escalation phrases)
**Daily Practice:** Call a time-out BEFORE flooding, then return
**Assessment:** Repair Attempts Inventory

---

## PHASE 3: EMOTIONAL DEPTH (Weeks 9-12)
*"Underneath Every Conflict Is Unmet Emotion"*

### Week 9: The Demon Dialogues
**Expert:** Susan Johnson (EFT)
**Theme:** The negative cycles YOU get stuck in
**Skills:**
- Find the Bad Guy, Protest Polka, Freeze & Flee
- YOUR role in the cycle (pursuer or withdrawer?)
- Naming the cycle together
**Daily Practice:** Catch the cycle starting → name it out loud
**Assessment:** Cycle Identification

### Week 10: Raw Spots & Attachment Injuries
**Expert:** Susan Johnson
**Theme:** The old wounds that get triggered
**Skills:**
- Identify YOUR raw spots (abandonment, rejection, inadequacy)
- Trace them to origin (childhood, past relationships)
- Communicate vulnerability without blame
**Daily Practice:** "When X happens, I feel Y because of Z in my past"
**Assessment:** Raw Spots Mapping

### Week 11: Vulnerability as Strength
**Expert:** Brené Brown
**Theme:** The courage to be seen
**Skills:**
- Shame vs. guilt distinction
- The vulnerability paradox (weakness = strength)
- Daring greatly in your relationship
**Daily Practice:** Share one vulnerable truth with partner
**Assessment:** Vulnerability Comfort Scale

### Week 12: Differentiation
**Expert:** Jennifer Finlayson-Fife
**Theme:** Growing up in your relationship
**Skills:**
- Self-confrontation (looking at YOUR part)
- Holding onto yourself while staying connected
- Not needing validation to know your worth
**Daily Practice:** Do something hard that your best self would do
**Assessment:** Differentiation Level

---

## PHASE 4: INTIMACY & DESIRE (Weeks 13-14)
*"Connection Beyond Words"*

### Week 13: Erotic Intelligence
**Expert:** Esther Perel + Jennifer Finlayson-Fife
**Theme:** Desire in long-term relationships
**Skills:**
- The erotic vs. the domestic
- Maintaining mystery and novelty
- Desire as wanting, not just willingness
**Daily Practice:** Create anticipation, break routine
**Assessment:** Desire Discrepancy Check

### Week 14: The Couple Bubble
**Expert:** Stan Tatkin
**Theme:** Being each other's "go-to" person
**Skills:**
- Secure functioning (we protect each other)
- Co-regulation of nervous systems
- Rituals of connection
**Daily Practice:** Check in morning and evening (eyes, touch, words)
**Assessment:** Couple Bubble Strength

---

## PHASE 5: INTEGRATION (Weeks 15-16)
*"Bringing It All Together"*

### Week 15: State Management & Influence
**Expert:** Tony Robbins + Jordan Belfort
**Theme:** Mastering YOUR state to lead the relationship
**Skills:**
- Physiology drives psychology (posture, breath, movement)
- Certainty, enthusiasm, rapport
- Leading by going first
**Daily Practice:** Set your state before important conversations
**Assessment:** Emotional Leadership Score

### Week 16: The Sound Relationship House
**Expert:** John Gottman (Integration)
**Theme:** The complete architecture
**Skills:**
- Love Maps (know your partner's world)
- Fondness & Admiration
- Shared Meaning (dreams, rituals, roles, goals)
**Daily Practice:** Full system review — which floors need work?
**Assessment:** Final Comprehensive Assessment + Progress Review

---

## PERSONALIZATION LOGIC

Based on assessment results, the app emphasizes certain weeks:

| Assessment Result | Extra Focus Weeks |
|------------------|-------------------|
| Anxious Attachment | 2, 8, 10, 12 (self-soothing, raw spots, differentiation) |
| Avoidant Attachment | 2, 7, 11, 14 (bids, vulnerability, couple bubble) |
| High Criticism | 5, 6, 7 (horsemen, empathy, bids) |
| Low Desire | 12, 13 (differentiation, erotic intelligence) |
| Communication Issues | 5, 6, 7, 8 (entire Phase 2) |
| Connection Issues | 9, 10, 14 (demon dialogues, raw spots, couple bubble) |
