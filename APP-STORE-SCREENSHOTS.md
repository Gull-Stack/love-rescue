# Love Rescue — App Store Screenshot Specs

## Required Sizes

| Device Display | Resolution | Aspect Ratio |
|---|---|---|
| 6.7" (iPhone 15 Pro Max / 16 Pro Max) | 1290 × 2796 | Required |
| 6.5" (iPhone 14 Plus / 13 Pro Max) | 1284 × 2778 | Required |
| 5.5" (iPhone 8 Plus) | 1242 × 2208 | Required if supporting older devices |

## Recommended: 6 Screenshots Per Size

### Screenshot 1 — Hero
**"Discover Your Relationship DNA"**
- Show the assessment overview/home screen
- Prominent gradient branding, tagline overlay
- Sets the tone for the entire listing

### Screenshot 2 — Assessment In Action
**"12 Science-Backed Assessments"**
- Show a quiz question mid-flow (e.g., Attachment Style)
- Clean UI, progress indicator visible

### Screenshot 3 — Results & Scores
**"Understand Your Patterns"**
- Show a completed assessment result with score visualization
- Radar chart or score breakdown

### Screenshot 4 — Daily Tracking
**"Track Your Connection Daily"**
- Show the daily tracker dashboard
- Mood/connection entries, streak indicator

### Screenshot 5 — Matchup Score
**"See How You Match"**
- Show the partner matchup comparison screen
- Side-by-side scores, compatibility percentage

### Screenshot 6 — Partner Features
**"Grow Together"**
- Show partner invite or shared dashboard
- Strategies and tips view

## Production Notes
- Screenshots must be captured from the live app running on iOS Simulator
- Use Xcode → Simulator → File → Screenshot (⌘S)
- Add marketing text overlays in Figma, Sketch, or a screenshot builder tool (e.g., Screenshots.pro, AppLaunchpad)
- Ensure no personal/test data is visible
- Match the app's gradient branding in frame/overlay design
