What Is the Gottman Method?

The Gottman Method is an approach to couples therapy developed by Drs. John and Julie Gottman, based on 40+ years of research with over 3,000 couples. Unlike approaches based on theory alone, the Gottman Method is built on observable data about what actually makes relationships succeed or fail.

The Four Horsemen

These four patterns predict divorce with over 90% accuracy:

1. Criticism

Attacking your partner's character rather than addressing a specific behavior. "You always..." or "You never..." statements.

2. Contempt

The most destructive pattern. Includes eye-rolling, mockery, name-calling, and hostile humor. Communicates disgust and superiority.

3. Defensiveness

Playing the victim, making excuses, or counter-attacking instead of taking responsibility.

4. Stonewalling

Withdrawing from interaction, shutting down, or giving the silent treatment. Often a response to emotional flooding.
